About Me

This section introduces my academic background, interests, and goals within construction technology, artificial intelligence, and facilities management.

Professional Profile

Accomplished Operations Manager with over 15 years of experience managing stadium operations, vendors, budgeting, project coordination, and large-scale event logistics at Bank of America Stadium. My background includes project management, facilities operations, contract administration, procurement, AutoCAD, and operational planning for NFL games, concerts, and major events.

Throughout my career with the Carolina Panthers organization, I have managed vendor operations, stadium conversions, capital projects, maintenance planning, and operational compliance while supporting high-profile national events including multiple NFL Super Bowls. My professional experience has strengthened my interest in how artificial intelligence, BIM technologies, automation, and predictive analytics can improve construction workflows and facilities management operations.

I am currently expanding my expertise through coursework in AI in Construction and Facilities Management, focusing on the future integration of AI technologies into construction project delivery, smart buildings, and operational efficiency.
